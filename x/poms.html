Post-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students

About the Scholarship

The Post-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students is a government-funded scholarship program aimed at providing financial assistance to students belonging to Scheduled Castes (SC) and Scheduled Tribes (ST) communities pursuing higher education at the post-matriculation level. This scholarship is designed to support their educational aspirations and reduce financial barriers.

Key Features

  • Open to students belonging to SC/ST communities pursuing higher education after completing their matriculation (class 10).
  • Provides financial support to cover tuition fees, maintenance allowances, and other educational expenses.
  • Encourages students to pursue undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional courses.
  • Helps in promoting education and enhancing the opportunities for SC/ST students.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Post-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Must belong to the Scheduled Caste (SC) or Scheduled Tribe (ST) category.
  • Should be pursuing higher education at the post-matriculation level in a recognized institution.
  • Family income should be within the specified limits as per the scholarship guidelines.
  • Additional eligibility requirements may apply, so it's essential to refer to the official scholarship guidelines for specific details.

Application Process

Interested candidates can apply for the Post-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students through the official scholarship portal provided by the government. Application periods, requirements, and deadlines may vary, so applicants should check the official website or contact the scholarship authorities for up-to-date information.

For More Information

For detailed information, eligibility criteria, and application guidelines, please visit the official scholarship portal or contact the scholarship authorities directly.
