Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students

About the Scholarship

The Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students is a government-funded scholarship program aimed at providing financial assistance to students belonging to Scheduled Castes (SC) and Scheduled Tribes (ST) communities at the pre-matriculation level. This scholarship aims to support their education and reduce the dropout rate among disadvantaged students.

Key Features

  • Open to students belonging to SC/ST communities studying in classes 9 and 10.
  • Provides financial support to cover educational expenses such as tuition fees, books, and stationery.
  • Encourages students to continue their education and pursue higher studies.
  • Helps in bridging the educational gap among disadvantaged communities.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Must belong to the Scheduled Caste (SC) or Scheduled Tribe (ST) category.
  • Should be studying in classes 9 or 10 in a recognized school.
  • Family income should be within the specified limits as per the scholarship guidelines.
  • Additional eligibility requirements may apply, so it's essential to refer to the official scholarship guidelines for specific details.

Application Process

Interested candidates can apply for the Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST Students through the official scholarship portal provided by the government. Application periods, requirements, and deadlines may vary, so applicants should check the official website or contact the scholarship authorities for up-to-date information.

For detailed information, eligibility criteria, and application guidelines, please visit the official scholarship portal or contact the scholarship authorities directly.
