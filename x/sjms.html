Suvarna Jubilee Merit Scholarship

About the Scholarship

The Suvarna Jubilee Merit Scholarship is a prestigious scholarship program offered by the Kerala State Government to recognize and reward exceptional academic achievements of students in the state.

Key Features

  • Available to students who have achieved full A+ grades in all subjects of the SSLC (Secondary School Leaving Certificate) examination in Kerala.
  • Financial support to pursue higher education.
  • Assists in covering tuition fees, purchasing books, and meeting other educational expenses.
  • Encourages students to strive for academic excellence.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Suvarna Jubilee Merit Scholarship, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Have achieved full A+ grades in all subjects of the SSLC examination conducted by the Kerala State Education Board.
  • Be pursuing higher education in a recognized college or university in Kerala.
  • Meet any additional criteria specified in the official scholarship guidelines.

Application Process

Interested candidates can apply for the Suvarna Jubilee Merit Scholarship through the official government website or portal. The application period, requirements, and deadlines may vary, so it's essential to check the official website for the most up-to-date information.

For More Information

For detailed information, eligibility criteria, and application guidelines, please visit the official Kerala State Government website or the National Scholarship Portal.
