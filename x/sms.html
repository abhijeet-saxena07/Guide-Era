Kerala State Merit Scholarship

About the Scholarship

The Kerala State Merit Scholarship is a prestigious scholarship program offered by the Kerala State Government to recognize and reward outstanding academic achievements of students in the state.

Key Features

  • Open to students who have secured top ranks in the SSLC and Plus Two examinations conducted by the Kerala State Education Board.
  • Financial support to pursue higher education.
  • Helps cover tuition fees, books, and other educational expenses.
  • Encourages students to excel academically and strive for excellence.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Kerala State Merit Scholarship, students must:

  • Have secured top ranks in the SSLC and Plus Two examinations in Kerala.
  • Be pursuing higher education in a recognized college or university.
  • Meet other specific criteria as outlined in the official scholarship guidelines.

Application Process

Interested candidates can apply for the scholarship through the official government website or portal. The application period, requirements, and deadlines may vary, so it's essential to check the official website for the latest information.

For More Information

For detailed information, eligibility criteria, and application guidelines, please visit the official Kerala State Government website or the National Scholarship Portal.
